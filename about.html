The Founder

Meet Mike Mears

Mike Mears is a California-based creative entrepreneur with a passion for bringing people together through shared experiences in film, art, and entertainment.

With a background as a comedian, writer, and celebrity panel moderator, Mike has spent years working at the intersection of entertainment and fan engagement. His experience moderating celebrity panels at major events gave him unique insight into what makes these interactions truly memorable.

As a writer, Mike co-wrote an award-winning indie thriller that earned the Top Indie Film Awards 2016 for Best Feature, along with nominations for Best Director, Best Actor, and Best Humor. This hands-on experience in film production deepened his appreciation for the craft and the artists behind the camera.

Mike's creative ventures also include developing an original 36-issue comic book series, showcasing his commitment to storytelling across multiple mediums.

Looking Ahead

Our Vision

Paralogic Presents was born from a simple idea: fans deserve more than just a fleeting moment with their favorite artists. They deserve experiences that honor both the art and the audience.

We're building a platform for multi-day events that combine film screenings, art exhibitions, live discussions, and intimate meet-and-greets. Each event is carefully curated to celebrate the legacy of beloved films and the talented individuals who brought them to life.

Our inaugural event featuring Bruce Davison represents just the beginning. We're committed to creating a series of events that bring together passionate fans and the artists who have shaped cinema history.
