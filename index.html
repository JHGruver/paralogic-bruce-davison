About the Artist

Bruce Davison

Emmy and Golden Globe-winning actor, acclaimed painter, and the star of iconic films including X-Men and Willard.

1971

Willard

Lead Role

Bruce's breakout performance as the lonely outsider who befriends a colony of rats became a cult classic and launched his career.

2000

X-Men

Senator Robert Kelly

A pivotal role in the blockbuster that launched the modern superhero film era, showcasing Bruce's range in the Marvel universe.

Accomplished Painter

Beyond the screen, Bruce is a talented visual artist whose paintings reflect the same depth and emotion he brings to his performances.
